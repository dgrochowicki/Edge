## Match Notes

### GenOne vs ex-RUSTEC

GenOne was added mainly as a low-odds leg intended to increase the total coupon odds.

The selection won, but it did not provide meaningful value on its own.

### The Last Resort vs QUAZAR

This was the main value selection on the coupon.

The Last Resort lost the match 1:2. The deciding map ended 4:13.

### Just Players vs Entropy

Just Players was another low-odds addition.

The team lost 0:2, which caused the entire coupon to fail.

## Post-Match Review

The coupon exposed a structural mistake in the process:

> Low-odds selections should not be added only to improve the combined coupon price.

Both 1.18 and 1.13 legs offered limited upside while increasing the number of ways the coupon could lose.

## Decision Added to the Playbook

- No fillers.
- Every selection must be strong enough to stand as a single bet.
- A low price is not evidence of low risk.
- Coupon construction must not reduce the quality threshold applied to each leg.
